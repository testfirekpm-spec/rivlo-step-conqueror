

## Recommendations to Make Rivlo Stand Out

Based on the current site structure, here are high-impact additions ordered by visual impact:

### 1. FAQ Accordion Section
Collapsible FAQ section before the footer using Radix accordion. Common questions about the app, pricing, compatibility. Adds credibility and reduces bounce.

### 2. Pricing / Plans Section
Three-tier pricing cards (Free, Pro, Team) with a glass card style, highlighted "popular" tier, and toggle for monthly/yearly. Positioned between Features and Testimonials.

### 3. Interactive App Screenshots Gallery
A tabbed or carousel showcase of actual app screens (leaderboard, achievements, hike tracking) with smooth crossfade transitions. More compelling than the single phone mockup.

### 4. Social Proof Bar
A thin banner near the hero showing real-time-style counters: "12,847 people walking right now" with a subtle pulse animation. Creates urgency and community feel.

### 5. Animated Gradient Dividers
Replace hard section breaks with flowing SVG wave or gradient mesh dividers that shift color subtly, inspired by Linear's section transitions.

### 6. Video/GIF Demo Section
A short looping video or animated GIF showing the app in action — scrolling through leaderboards, unlocking achievements. Embedded in a device frame with autoplay.

### 7. Achievement Badges Showcase
A visual grid of unlockable badges/achievements (e.g., "10K Steps", "7-Day Streak", "Summit Reached") with hover reveals. Gamification is the core value prop — show it off.

### 8. Dark/Light Mode Toggle
Adding a theme toggle in the navbar signals polish and modern UX sensibility.

---

**Recommended priority order for maximum impact:**
1. FAQ Section (quick win, adds depth)
2. Pricing Section (conversion driver)
3. Achievement Badges Showcase (reinforces gamification USP)
4. Animated Gradient Dividers (visual polish)
